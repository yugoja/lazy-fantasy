## Design Context

### Users
Young sports fans (20s-30s), primarily Indian cricket fans during IPL season, expanding to football (World Cup 2026) and F1. Using the app on mobile, checking daily during tournament season. Competing with real-life friend groups — the app is an extension of group chat banter.

### Brand Personality
Cheeky, competitive, social. The app should feel like trash-talking your mates, not placing bets. It's a game among friends, not a gambling platform.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone**: Playful and punchy — not corporate, not clinical, not generic
- **Anti-references**: Dream11 (too complex/betting-adjacent), standard AI-generated dark dashboards (v0/shadcn defaults), betting apps
- **Theme**: Dark mode primary, mobile-first (430px max)
- **Fonts**: Inter (body) + Space Grotesk (brand/display)
- **Palette**: Deep navy bg, green primary (#26d666), orange/gold accent (#f39c3a)

### Design Principles
1. **Friends first, stats second** — every screen should feel social, not analytical
2. **Punchy over polished** — bold choices, strong opinions, personality in the details
3. **Cricket native, sport agnostic** — design system must flex to new sports without cricket-specific assumptions
4. **Mobile muscle** — every interaction designed for one-handed thumb use, no desktop afterthoughts
5. **Not another AI app** — if it looks like v0 generated it, redesign it
