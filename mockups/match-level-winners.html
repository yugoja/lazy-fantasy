Lazy Fantasy · Cricket · Studio notes

Per-match winners, not just a season trophy.

Today the leaderboard is a single number per player that ticks up silently across the season. Match-level winners turn each fixture into its own moment — bragging rights, a screenshot, a reason to open the group chat.

What the system already does

Per-match scoring · max 140 pts
10 (winner) + 4 × 20 (top runs / top wickets, per team) + 50 (POM)
Tournament-level picks
Top-4 teams + best batsman + best bowler, scored once the tournament ends
League leaderboard
Season cumulative — sums per-match points and tournament picks for every member

What's missing

Match-level recognition. When a fixture ends, every member's per-match score is calculated and quietly added to their season total. Nobody is named the "winner of match 24." Four design takes below — each surfaces the missing moment, with a clear sense of where it would live in the app.

01 / 04

Match Verdict

Lives on · dashboard Tone · refined / editorial Density · high

A single card that lands on the dashboard the moment a match resolves. Names the winner with editorial weight, shows the four-category hit list that got them there, and stacks the runners-up below. Closest to the existing app's voice — would ship most easily.

02 / 04

Judges' Scorecard

Lives on · match result screen Tone · sport-broadcast / boxing Density · highest — full league grid

A judges' card. Every member as a row, four prediction categories as columns, a red dot for every cell they hit. The total column declares the winner with a CHAMP tab. Most informative — readable at a glance who got what — but heavier to ship and harder to fit when leagues grow beyond ~8 members.

03 / 04

MVP Trading Card

Lives on · profile + share sheet Tone · collectible / premium Density · low — single hero

Every match win mints a numbered card. Holographic foil for flawless games, plain finish for ordinary wins. Stacks up in the user's profile as a season-long collection — long-term retention play. Easy to share; the back of the card carries a generated caption. Hover the card to see it settle.

04 / 04

Match Receipt

Lives on · share sheet / WhatsApp drop Tone · lo-fi / utility Density · medium — itemised breakdown

Every match prints a receipt. Itemised points per member, perforated edges, paper grain, barcode at the foot. Designed to be screenshot and dropped in the group chat intact — feels less like a brag and more like a record. Most distinct visual language of the four; would also work well as a shareable PDF post-match.
